序章 SAYDHI’S ESTATE

Isa: (So you’ve done it. You killed the God King. Congratulations.

Isa: You do realize that now every deathless in the world is going to come hunting you. For that blade.

Siris: I’ll find someone to help me. Maybe someone to take the sword off my hands.

Siris: The Worker of Secrets…

Isa: The Worker is a myth. Pure fabrication.

Isa: Nobody fights back against the Deathless. Nobody.

Siris: I did.

Isa: You’re committed to this foolish course?

Siris: I am.

Isa: Saydhi. She’s one of the Deathless–an information dealer.

Isa: If anyone knows where the Worker of Secrets is, it will be her…

Siris: What do I have to do?

Isa: Saydhi has a standing invitation. She loves duels.

Isa: Any man who can best her champions wins a prize.

Isa: Fight your way to her, and she’ll answer a question for you.

Isa: Siris?

Isa: Good luck. Maybe if something goes wrong, I can…

Siris: Don’t get yourself killed, Isa.

Isa: I’ll promise if you’ll do the same.

Siris: It’s a deal then.


Saydhi: Impressive! Who taught you the Aegis forms, Warrior?

Siris: I have come for my prize. I wish a question answered.

Saydhi: Something so… pedestrian.

Saydhi: You could be my new champion, and find glory in battle.

Saydhi: Riches, women, and power would be yours for the taking.

Siris: A question.

Saydhi: Very well. What great mystery does your small mind ponder?

Siris: Where can I find the Worker of Secrets?

Saydhi: Whose child are you? Which immortal’s blood do you have in your veins?

Siris: Answer my question.

Saydhi: The Vault of Tears. The place once known as Saranthia - due west, past the sea, in the mountains to the north.

Saydhi: You could find him there.

Saydhi: But you won’t.


Raidriar: Yes… I thought you might fall for that. You opened the pathway to my dungeons, after all.

Raidriar: I do believe she was planning on taking the sword and betraying me.

Raidriar: A pity, but I suppose you did me a favor by killing her.

Siris: I…

Siris: So you DO still live. And you know what I am. You sought out my lineage.

Raidriar: Oh, I know what you are. Though I am more and more certain that you do not.

Raidriar: Thank you for returning this to me.

Siris: Give me a sword. Fight me!

Raidriar: I think not. You… surprised me, last time. I don’t think I’ll put myself in that position again.

Raidriar: You really don’t know what you are, do you? Ironic - what DID you do yourself, Ausar?

REBIRTH 1

(gasp)

Siris: What… What is happening? How am I alive?

Siris: Worker, I will free you.


Thane: You are a fool to come here, mortal - this place holds nothing but death for one such as you.

Siris: It holds the Worker of Secrets. I have come to free him. If you stand in my way, I will have your head.

Thane: Mwa-hah-ha! No one alive can release him.

Thane: He is bound by the blood of the Deathless, sealed by the abominations of his own make, and safeguarded by Sentinels of the ruling four.

Thane: I told you… that there is nothing here for you, Mortal.

Thane: Do you not hear me, mortal? If you open that seal, it will cost your blood - your life.

Thane: You will die!

Siris: You mistake me.

Thane: And how is that, little man.

Siris: You assume that I am mortal.

Thane: …

REBIRTH 2

Siris: I’m alive again. Hell take me… I’m one of them, aren’t I?

Siris: Worker, I will free you.


Archivist ( Blood Sentinel 1 ): You should not be here.

Siris: What IS this place?

Archivist: It was once the home of the most vile evil ever to befall this world.

Archivist: He’s been gone for ages… slain by Raidriar on the plains of Koroth.

Archivist: Many Devoted believe him truly dead - yet here I study, ponder, and… question.

Archivist: The seals must never be broken, for he cannot be allowed to return.

Archivist: Poor little man. You are a fraud, and likely a pawn.

Archivist: I spill your blood with regret.

Archivist: This cannot be! Ausar… ?

Archivist: You’ve begun something… you cannot finish, Ausar.

Archivist: This place will come crashing down… before you can unlock them all. And the worker… will die with it!

REBIRTH 3

Siris: All those years, going to face the God King in his palace… each time, each bloodline, was me.

Siris: Worker, I will free you.

REBIRTH 4

Siris: I don’t care if I’m one of them. I will free the mankind, I will do what is right.

Siris: Worker, I will free you.


( MX-Goliath - Blood Sentinel 3)

Goliath: Who goes there?

Siris: Who are you? You have to get out of this place. It’s dangerous.

Goliath: The others are dead, then? And you their killer?

Goliath: You don’t look like a great warrior.

Siris: Apparently so. You’re human. You shouldn’t serve them.

Siris: You should fight. They CAN be defeated.

Galiath: I care little for their squabbles, wanderer.

Galiath: I have a higher purpose, to uphold the Great Pact, and defend this Tower from any who should violate it.

Galiath: Lest they bring damnation and death upon us all.

Siris: So you’ll make me kill you too.

Galiath: You make that sound as if it will be an easy task.

REBIRTH 5

Siris: Worker, I will free you.


Siris: I saw you die.

Thane: And I you. So, you are one of us, are you? Deathless.

Siris: I will free the worker - and with these weapons he forged, I will end your ‘immortal’ life.

Thane: You think to betray us, and harvest the Worker’s secrets for yourself?

Thane: Then you are more of a fool than I imagined.

Thane: You will not unlock this vault, traitor, no matter how many times you are reborn.

Thane: For I am Thane - first blood of the Deathless, high lord of house Ix, and shield of the Great Pact.

Thane: I will destroy your body, hunt down your bloodline, and imprison your soul for all time!

结局 THE GOD KING

Thane: You… will not… release… the Worker!


Siris: How did you…?

Worker: I know a great many things about the Deathless - including how to destabilize their Quantum Identity Pattern, granting them a true Death.

Siris: You are him! The Worker of Secrets!

Worker: Ausar! Is that you? Have you at last come to release me as you promised you would so many millennia ago?

Siris: I have little memory of who I was before. I’m not certain I wish anymore than I have.

Siris: I am known as Siris now.

Worker: Curious - a fate nearly worse than mine, it seems.

Worker: Tell me Siris, who was once my friend Ausar - why is it you have to come to my prison?

Siris: I’m here to free you. You once set out to destroy the Deathless and free our world.

Siris: I share the same goal, but I cannot do it alone.

Worker: Indeed. Time runs short, my friend. But only Raidriar can release me from this place.

Siris: Raidriar?

Worker:I believe those he enslaves refer to him as “the God King”

Worker: You must defeat him, and bring him here - he must take my place if I am to go free.

Worker: Until then, my soul is trapped in this place. I cannot step from this tomb; walls of spirit confine me.

Siris: That is no easy task. I bested him once, but he knows what I am now.

Siris: I fear I’ll not be able to defeat him a second time.

Worker: You must have fought through Heaven and Hell to find me.

Worker: Do this one last thing, and I will help you free this world from the tyrants who possess it.

Siris: How will I find the God-King?

Worker: You are his most ancient enemy, Ausar. With the Infinity Blade active, he will do all in his power to destroy you.

Worker: Simply go where Raidriar can find you, and he will come.

Siris: And all I have to do is defeat him but not kill him?

Siris: Hell take me.

Siris: Very well.


Siris: Raidriar!!! Come and face me, you coward!

Siris: I’ll be waiting.


Raidriar: So you return, Ausar. Mwa-hah-ha!

Raidriar: Oh, ever the fool. You won’t escape me again.

Raidriar: Take him, but be sure to leave the killing blow for my blade.


Raidriar: I have killed you hundreds of times, but now, finally, you will truly taste death, Ausar!

Siris: We shall see.


Raidriar: You always were… a vile, plotting wretch… Ausar.

Raidriar: Killing me will not end this, you know.

Siris: What makes you think I will let you die that easily?


Siris: I defeated Raidriar - he can take your place in this eternal prison.

Worker: Excellent! Heh-heh. It looks like he gave you quite a fight.

Worker: Quickly my friend, release me, and together we will re-shape this world!

Worker: Oh, did I forget to mention? For me to leave this prison, another Deathless must take my place.

Worker: I must thank you, Ausar - not only have you freed me, but you’ve eliminated many of my rivals for this world.

Worker: Those who remain will follow me…

Worker: Or I will take from them that which they hold most dear - their eternal life.

Worker: Unfortunately, for me to escape, “old friend,” I’ll have to let you keep your life.You can spend an eternity in this cell.

Worker: You and Raidriar can keep each other company.

Worker: It’s only a fair, after all, YOU were the one who imprisoned me here to begin with.

Siris: No… What have I done?! Noooooooooooooooooooooooo!!!

THE HOLY BAND

Siris: TEL, why are you here? I told you not to enter the tower unless you have to retrieve my body for Rebirth.

TEL: Master, you must not enter the tomb!

Siris: Why not? What’s down there?

TEL: I cannot say - I can only tell you that misery awaits you below!

Siris: I have to see for myself.


TEL: You were not supposed to see this, Master. These memories were supposed to stay hidden. Oh, this is not good, not good at all…
